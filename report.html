Banks vs. Fintechs: Strategic Differences

Traditional Banks prioritize trust and support, while Fintechs focus on speed and low friction.

What Banks Prioritize

  • Chat & Support (100% vs 72%): Banks offer callbacks, WhatsApp, and live agents.
  • Testimonials (61.5% vs 36%): Banks showcase reviews to build institutional trust.
  • Use-Case Targeting (61.5% vs 44%): Banks offer specific variants (Wedding/Travel loans).

What Fintechs Prioritize

  • Collateral-Free (80% vs 69.2%): Fintechs emphasize "no security required".
  • Step-by-Step Flow (72% vs 61.5%): Fintechs walk users through the online apply wizard.
  • Instant Disbursal (92% vs 84.6%): Fintechs focus on speed (e.g., "within 15 minutes").
