Personal Loan Eligibility

Here’s what you need to understand about Shriram Personal Loan’s eligibility criteria to enhance your chances of approval:

Eligibility Criteria

Residence

  • You must be an Indian resident

Age

  • Minimum 21 years at application; maximum 60 years at loan closure

Employment Type

  • Both salaried and self-employed are eligible. Please note that if you’re self-employed, you may be eligible for a business loan, or you must be a pre-approved customer with an existing relationship with Shriram Finance to qualify for a personal loan.

Personal Loan FAQs

What is a personal loan?

A personal loan from Shriram Finance is a collateral-free or unsecured loan that allows you fund a variety of your financial needs easily at a fixed interest rate and over a fixed period of time. Interest rates for a Shriram Personal Loan start at 11%* p.a. and tenure options range from 12 to 60 months.

How does a personal loan work?

A personal loan works by providing you with a lump sum amount of money upfront, which you then have to repay over a period through regular equated monthly instalments or EMIs. To apply for the loan, you need to first meet all the eligibility criteria set by Shriram Finance. Once approved, your desired loan amount will be credited to your bank account. You then have to make consistent payments each month towards your loan until the entire amount is paid off.

How can I get a personal loan?

You can get a personal loan from Shriram Finance by visiting the official website or the Shriram One app, registering with your mobile number, entering your personal details and uploading your documents. A loan executive from Shriram Finance will then reach out to you to take the process forward.

How much personal loan can I get?

At Shriram Finance, you can get a personal loan up to ₹10,00,000, depending on your eligibility.

How can I repay my personal loan?

You can repay your personal loan typically through bank transfers, cheques, online payments or auto-debit from your bank account.

How can I check the status of a personal loan?

You can check the status of your personal loan by contacting Shriram Finance’s customer care, online through our website or via our Shriram One app.

What are the tenure options for a personal loan?

The tenure options for a personal loan from Shriram Finance ranges from 12 to 60 months.

What are the advantages of taking a personal loan from Shriram Finance?

Some of the advantages of taking a personal loan from Shriram Finance are attractive interest rates, flexible tenure, quick approvals and minimal documentation.

How can I check my loan balance?

You can check your loan balance by logging in to the Shriram Finance website or by contacting customer service. You can also download the Shriram One app to manage your loan account.

Can I apply for multiple personal loans?

Yes, you can apply for multiple personal loans, but usually it is not recommended. Applying for too many loans at the same time can make you seem desperate for credit and damage your credit score as well.
