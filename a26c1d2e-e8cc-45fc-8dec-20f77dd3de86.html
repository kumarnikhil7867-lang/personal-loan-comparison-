    Eligibility & Documentation for Instant Personal Loans Online

    Learn about the eligibility requirements and necessary documentation for quick loan approval.

    Features

    • Loan amount from ₹6,000 to ₹10 Lakhs (*maximum amount may be contingent upon applicant’s employment and financial status)
    • Interest Rates ranging from 12% to 28% per annum
    • Tenure ranging from 6 months to 60 months
    • Processing fee up to 4.8% + GST
    • Quick & direct transfer to the Bank Account
    • No collateral or pledge of security

    Eligibility Criteria

    • Indian Citizen
    • Age to be 21 years and above
    • Minimum monthly personal income of ₹10,000
    • Monthly household income more than ₹25,000
    • Possess Government-approved Valid Identity Proof & Address Proof
    • Aadhaar-linked mobile number
    • Active employee with regular/permanent status of employment (for salaried employees)
    • 2 months of work experience in your current company (for salaried employees)

    Documents Required

    • Photograph
    • PAN
    • Address Proof (Permanent & Current Address)
    • Income Proof (Bank Statements reflecting Monthly Income)
    • Employment Proof for salaried employees (Salary Slip / Office ID / UAN authentication)

    Other Fee & Charges

      • Foreclosure charges
        • Foreclosure charges of upto 5% of principal outstanding* + GST will be charged in case Foreclosure is opted
      • Part-Prepayment Charges
        • Part Prepayment Charges of upto 5% of principal prepaid* + GST will be charged in case Part-Prepayment is opted
      • Penal Charges
        • 1. EMI bounce charge (1st day): 4% of principal overdue* or ₹500, whichever is lower
        • 2. Penal charges for 2-180 days: 36% per annum on principal overdue
      • Credit Information Report Fees
        • 1. Credit Information Report Fees of ₹50 + GST is applied if the customer opts for receiving their Credit Information Report.
        • 2. Credit Information Report fees is disclosed upfront in the KFS (Key Fact Statement).
      • Retained Processing Fees in case of cool-off
        • 1. A percentage of the one-time processing fees may be retained as the cool off fee if the customer exits the loan during the cooling-off period.
        • 2. The exact amount of the cool off fees is disclosed upfront in the KFS (Key Fact Statement).
      • Stamp Duty
        • 1. Stamp duty charges upto ₹ 1000
        • 2. The exact amount of the Stamp duty charges is disclosed upfront in the KFS (Key Fact Statement).
      • Maintenance & Procurement Cost
        • Maintenance & Procurement charges upto ₹ 14 + GST
      • *round to the nearest rupee
